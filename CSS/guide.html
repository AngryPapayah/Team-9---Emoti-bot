Emoti-Guide

If you're feeling down, Emoti is here to calm.

Set up


1. Place the Emoti-sens near the front door with the sensors facing the opposite wall.
2. Position the Emoti-bot near the entrance (at least 3 meters away from the door).
3. Turn on the Emoji-bot/-sens.
4. When you enter, the Emoti-sens will send an infrared signal to the Emoti-bot, activating it.
5. The Emoti-bot will drive towards the person and make sure they feel appreciated and loved.
6. Have fun!
